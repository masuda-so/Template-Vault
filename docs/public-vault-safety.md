# Public Vault Safety

> This document is an engineering compliance checklist, not legal advice.
> Before public release, paid distribution, user-data collection, or production operation, confirm the final position with a qualified professional.


## Scope

Template-Vault is a public Obsidian vault template. It should remain safe to clone, inspect, and reuse.

## Rules

- Samples must be fictional or generic.
- Do not include real customer names, private conversations, addresses, private emails, credentials, access tokens, or private project records.
- Do not include copyrighted long-form third-party content unless the license clearly permits redistribution.
- Keep the license and reuse terms visible.

## Checklist

- [ ] Sample notes are fictional.
- [ ] No private user data.
- [ ] No secrets.
- [ ] No copied copyrighted article/book content.
- [ ] License remains present.
